General Medicine

About This Service

Our General Medicine department provides comprehensive primary healthcare for adults. We focus on prevention, early diagnosis, and effective treatment of a wide range of health conditions. Our experienced general practitioners serve as your first point of contact for medical concerns.

  • Common illnesses (flu, colds, infections)
  • Chronic conditions (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
  • Preventive health screenings
  • Health assessments and annual check-ups

Laboratory Tests
